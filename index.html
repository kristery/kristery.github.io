About Me

I am a Ph.D. student advised by Xiaolong Wang at UC San Diego in the CSE department. I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University. In the past, I've worked with Hao Su at UCSD and Masashi Sugiyama at RIKEN-AIP. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University. My research interest lies in exploring machine learning, reinforcement learning and robotics for real-world decision-making problems such as object manipulation by considering the imperfectness of data and costly sampling conditions. Please see my CV for more information. If you would like to know more about my research, please contact me via email, kriswu8021 [at] gmail.com.
